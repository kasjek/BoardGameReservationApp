# Vision

## BoardGameReservationApp

The purpose of the app is to simplify the way boardgames players can find other people to play with and for the venue owners to manage table reservations in their cafes and restaurants.

### Goals

- Reserve tables in venues for a defined number of people, date and time
- Help venue owners manage capacity of their restaurants and cafes 

### Principles

- Clear over clever — reservation status should be obvious at a glance to all parties - users CREATING the table, users RESERVING a SEAT at an existing table, VENUE OWNERS and ADMINS
- Fast to book — get from interest to confirmed reservation in a few taps
- Fast to pay - to come at a later stage
- Trustworthy — availability of tables should be updated and visible to all users at any time
