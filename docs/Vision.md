# Vision

## BoardGameReservationApp

Make it simple for people to discover, reserve, and play board games together.

### Goals

- Reserve a table for a time slot without friction (first player experience)
- Browse available board games and see what is free to play
- Reserve games for a time slot without friction
- Help organizers and venues manage seating, inventory, and reservations

### Principles

- Clear over clever — reservation status should be obvious at a glance
- Fast to book — get from interest to confirmed reservation in a few taps
- Trustworthy — inventory and availability stay accurate in real time
